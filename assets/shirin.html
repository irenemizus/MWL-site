Sergei V. Shirin

E-mail: shirin@appl.sci-nnov.ru

Sergei V. Shirin worked in the Lab from 1997 to 2013.Was born on March 5th, 1979 in Nizhny Novgorod, Russia. Graduated from the Radio Physics Department of the Nizhny Novgorod State University in 2000.
Since 2000 has been involved in close cooperation with the group of Prof. Jonathan Tennyson from University College London.
In 2005 worked in cooperation with Universität Ulm (Germany) on Leonhard-Euler-Projekt. The theme of the Ph.D. thesis is “Fitting of the potential energy surface of water molecule and its application for experimental spectra analyses”

Main interests are theoretical spectroscopy, variational calculation, and computers.
