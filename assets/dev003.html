Instruments Devices Device 003

Device 003:

Backward-wave oscillator tubes installed in permanent magnet.

The small one - 0.65 Tesla Samarium-Cobalt alloy permanent magnet with adjustment mechanism for tubes of 180-260 GHz and 260-380 GHz range.

The bigger one - 1 Tesla Samarium-Cobalt magnet for tubes of higher frequency range up to 715 GHz.

0.68 Tesla Nd-Fe-B alloy permanent magnet with adjustment mechanism for tubes of 180-260 GHz and 260-380 GHz range.

1.1 Tesla Nd-Fe-B alloy permanent magnet with adjustment mechanism for high frequency tubes.

High frequency BWO adjustment mechanism for permanent magnet.
