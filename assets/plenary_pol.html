    3. 1998 – International Conference "Water in the Gas Phase", June 21–24, 1998, University of Marne la Vallee, Paris, France, "Analysis of rovibrational water spectra".
    4. 2003 – International Conference on High Accuracy Potentials for Quantum Dynamics, March 2003, University College London, UK, "High accuracy ab initio rotation vibration transitions of water".
    5. 2006 – International conference on High resolution Molecular Spectroscopy, Prague, "Water spectra at dissociation".
    6. 2011 – an invited talk at Royal Society meeting on "Water in the gas phase", "Global spectroscopy of water monomer".
    7. 2012 – an invited talk at Royal Society meeting on "Chemistry, astronomy and physics of H3+", "Spectroscopy of H3+ based on a new high accuracy global PES and DMS".
    8. 2012 – Highrus XVII Simposium on high resolution molecular spectroscopy, Zelenograd, "Global spectroscopy of small molecules".
