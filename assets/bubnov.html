Grigoriy Mikhailovich Bubnov

E-mail: bubnov@appl.sci-nnov.ru
Tel: +7 (908) 155-69-90

Grigoriy Bubnov was born 29.10.1989 in Gorky (now Nizhny Novgorod), Russia.

Graduated from Nizhny Novgorod State Technical University (NNSTU) in 2013 with master-engineer's degree of Radiotechnics. The theme of master's thesis is "MM-wave radiometer for atmospheric absorption measurements". Enrolled in postgraduate school of NNSTU in 2013. The field of scientific interests includes methods and techniques of millimeter-submillimeter wave spectroscopy and atmospheric radiometry, laboratory and field investigations of atmospheric absorption. Took part in a several expeditions of astroclimate research. Since 2011 to the present time works at the Institute of Applied Physics RAS. Current position is Junior Researcher.
