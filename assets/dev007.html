Instruments Devices Device 007

Device 007:

Submillimeter-wave radiation multiplier

Input wave-guide: 1.2 x 2.4 mm corresponding to output wave-guides of 180-260 and 260-380 GHz range BWOs. Wave-guide tuner for better performance at desired frequency point. Planar Schottky diode at the edge of narrowing ridged wave-guide. Horn antennae at the output. Bias is required. No filtering out of fundamental radiation. Observable harmonic numbers: up to 6.
