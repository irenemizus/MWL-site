Vladimir Yurievich Makhnev

E-mail: makhnev@ipfran.ru
Tel: +7 (831) 416-49-32

Vladimir Makhnev was born in Gorky region, July 17, 1992.

Graduated from the ASGAP (the Advanced School of General and Applied Physics) — faculty of Nizhny Novgorod State University — in 2015 as a master of physics (theme of work: «The study of the influence of stratospheric warming on the ozone structure in the middle atmosphere by microwave radiometry methods»). After that he started working at the Microwave Spectroscopy Laboratory of IAP RAS as a Junior Researcher. Since August, 2015 he is taking a PhD cource. The theme of PhD study is "Precision quantum mechanic calculations of molecular spectra for Solar system planets and exoplanets atmospheres research".
