... Microwave Spectroscopy
Perhaps the most important development in the Spectroscopy of the microwave region in this decade has been the discovery of microwave signals from molecules in interstellar dust clouds... ...Another technical development is the introduction of acoustic detection, especially in the millimeter region. Krupnov has reported very successful results with this means of detection, which also appears promising for low resolution Spectroscopy where higher pressure samples can then be advantageously used (with consequent increase in band intensity) without the limitation set by arcing in Stark detection...
