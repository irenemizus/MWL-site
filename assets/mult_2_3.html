The multiplier with 260-380 GHz range BWO (RAD gas cell filled with SO2)

Rotational lines of SO2 molecule observed using RAD gas cell at 2-nd, 3-rd and 5-th harmonics of frequency stabilized BWO radiation. Frequency of the BWO fundamental radiation in MHz is shown on the scale. (See Ref. 3 and 4 (1995) for more details). Intensity of molecular lines observed at harmonics or power of the harmonics essentially depends on Schtottky diode bias. Different harmonics require different bias for optimal generation.

The plot presents experimental record of ground state RQ4-branch of H2S2 molecule at 1258 GHz observed in 4-th harmonic of the 260-380 GHz BWO radiation.
