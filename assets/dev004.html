Device 004:

Gas Cell with Radio-Acoustic Detector (RAD) and related electronics for signal reception and amplification.

20 cm long and 2 cm in diameter tube with thin teflon windows (upper part of the device) connected to capacitance microphone (lower part of the device). Working gas pressure: 0.05-10 Torr. Acoustic signal frequency: 20-1000 Hz. Input microwave radiation range: from long millimeters up to far infrared wavelengths. Acoustic signal receiver: high frequency balanced bridge which includes the cell microphone capacitance. Typical single-pass sensitivity: 10-8 cm-1 in terms of radiation absorption coefficient (time constant 1sec).
