Main directions of activity Studies of the interactions

Studies of manifestations of molecular collisional interactions through lineshape, broadening, shifting and coupling of spectral lines

Methods developed in Laboratory permit accurate studies of collisional parameters of molecular lines. During the studies in the Laboratory the large collisional lineshifts, the effect of nonadditivity of collisional lineshifts in the system of molecular transitions and the second order collisional line coupling effects were observed for the first time. Collisional effects studies play important role in formation of atmospheric spectral lines of molecules. Combined studies by RAD and resonator spectrometers developed in the Laboratory permitted to reach new level of precision and reliability in the studies of lineshapes, broadening, shifting and integral intensity of atmospheric lines.
