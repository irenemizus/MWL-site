Instruments Devices Device 009

Device 009:

Set of devices for radio-acoustic spectroscopy.

  • Radiation source with power supply.
  • Multiplier-mixer and Synchronizer for the radiation source frequency stabilization.
  • High-vacuum gas cell with radio-acoustic detector and related signal receiving electronic.

Reference frequency source is also required. Depending on desired frequency range of the spectrometer it can be 2-20 GHz commercial synthesizer if the range of the spectrometer is below 400 GHz, or 78-118 GHz synthesizer for higher operating frequencies of the spectrometer.
