Geogry Fedoseev was born in N. Novgorod, Russia, January 1, 1990.

Graduated from the Radiophysical department of the Nizhny Novgorod State University in 2013 as a master of physics. The theme of the master diploma is «Analysis of physical mechanisms influencing on the shape of the molecular absorption lines». Started working in the Microwave Spectroscopy laboratory of the Institute of Applied Physics RAS in 2013 as a laboratory assistant. Since 2014 he is a PhD student.
