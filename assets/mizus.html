Irene I. Mizus (Bubukina) was born in Nizhny Novgorod, Russia, 17.03.1986.

Graduated from the ASGAP (the Advanced School of General and Applied Physics) — faculty of Nizhny Novgorod State University — in 2009 as a master of physics. The theme of the master diploma is «The H216O molecule PES optimization». Started working in the Microwave Spectroscopy laboratory (MWL) of the Institute of Applied Physics RAS (IAP RAS) in 2008 as a laboratory assistant, since 2009 till 2014 was a junior researcher in MWL. Since 2009 up to 2012 was taking a PhD course in postgraduate school of IAP RAS. The PhD thesis entitled «Analysis of H216O and H3+ spectra using global potential energy surfaces» was defended in 2014. Current position is a research associate in MWL.

Major interests: astrophysics, cosmology, theoretical molecular spectroscopy.
